eatr

Find food around you right now. eatr will find you the best restaurants around you FAST. Just open the app, pick one, and eat!

Try it

You can try v0.1 by downloading and installing the .apk file. This is a very early release and does contain dangerous workarounds and bugs. Use at your own risk.
